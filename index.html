HVAC engineering applied to commercial and industrial projects

Explore how technical clarity, HVAC design, ventilation planning, and system documentation support better project outcomes.

HVAC Modernization for Residential Condominium - Acapulco

Comprehensive rehabilitation of the air conditioning system following damage caused by Hurricane Otis. The project included 28 chillers, a chilled water pumping system, and more than 600 fan coil units, improving energy efficiency, reliability, and corrosion resistance in a coastal environment.

Controlled Cleanroom Facility - Guadalajara

Design and implementation of a complete HVAC solution for a 1,440 m² controlled environment facility. The system provides precise temperature, humidity, and air renewal control through air handling units, chilled water equipment, and SCADA-based automation.

Industrial Ventilation for Poultry Processing Plant

Design of mechanical ventilation and exhaust systems for critical production areas within a poultry processing facility. The solution supports sanitary control, worker safety, and compliance with the requirements necessary for federal TIF certification.

Carnot Project - Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Facility

Installation of HVAC and environmental control systems for laboratories, production areas, warehouses, and administrative spaces. The project was developed to meet strict pharmaceutical standards and regulatory requirements while ensuring controlled environmental conditions.

Control Center - Olmeca Refinery, Dos Bocas

Engineering and deployment of a redundant chilled water HVAC system for the main control center of one of Mexico's most strategic energy facilities. The solution delivers precise climate control, exceptional reliability, and uninterrupted operation for mission-critical systems.
